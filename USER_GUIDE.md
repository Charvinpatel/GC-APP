# 📖 TransportPro User Guide

Welcome to the **TransportPro** mobile app. This guide explains how to manage your transport fleet, track trips, monitor diesel usage, and handle finances.

---

## 🔐 Getting Started

### 1. Login
- Open the app and enter your **Email** and **Password**.
- The app uses **Secure Login**: Your session is saved, so you don't need to log in every time you open the app.
- **Role-Based Access**: Some features (like Trip Verification) are only visible to **Admins**.

---

## 📈 Analytics Dashboard
The first screen you see after logging in.
- **Top Statistics**: See Total Trips, Profit, and Revenue for Today.
- **Performance Chart**: A 7-day bar chart showing your fleet's activity over the past week.
- **Recent Trips**: Quickly view the last few trips recorded in the system.

---

## 🚜 Managing Trips

### 1. Recorded Trips (`Trips` Tab)
- View a complete history of **Verified Trips**.
- Use the **Search** and **Filter** buttons to find trips by Date, Driver, Vehicle, or Soil Type.
- **Add Trip**: Manually record a trip (Admin use only).
- **Edit/Delete**: Long-press or tap a trip to modify it.

### 2. Driver Trip Verification (`Driver Trips`)
- This is where trips submitted by drivers appear.
- **Review**: Check the details (Driver, Vehicle, Material, Start/End point).
- **Verify**: Tap "Verify" to approve the trip and move it to the master records.
- **Reject**: Delete or decline incorrect submissions.

---

## ⛽ Diesel & Fuel Management
- **Dashboard**: View recent diesel entries and total costs.
- **Add Entry**: Record fuel quantity, cost per unit, and the vehicle/driver involved.
- **History**: Track fuel consumption patterns over time.

---

## 💰 Finance & Upad (Advance)

### 1. Finance Summary
- A high-level overview of your business's financial health.
- Filter by **Last 7 Days**, **30 Days**, **90 Days**, or **1 Year**.
- View detailed breakdowns per Driver or Vehicle.

### 2. Upad / Advance Payments
- **DR (Debit)**: When you give an advance payment to a driver.
- **CR (Credit)**: When a driver returns money or it's deducted from their earnings.
- **Driver Balance**: Each driver's profile shows their current outstanding balance.

---

## 📊 Reports
Generate detailed reports for your business:
- **Daily Reports**: A summary of all activities (Trips & Diesel) for a specific day.
- **Driver Reports**: All work done by a specific driver over a date range.
- **Vehicle Reports**: A performance audit for any truck in your fleet.
- **Summary**: A consolidated report of all operations.

---

## 🗂️ Management (Masters)
Use these screens to set up and manage your fleet resources:
- **Drivers**: Add name, phone, license number, and status.
- **Vehicles**: Track your trucks by Plate Number and Type (e.g., Tipper, Dumper).
- **Soil Types**: Define materials (Sand, Soil, Gravel) and set their default rates.
- **Locations**: Manage common start and end points (Mining Site, Construction Site).
- **Vendors**: Track third-party contractors and clients.
- **Bills**: Generate and manage invoices for your clients.

---

## ⚙️ Profile & Settings
- **User Info**: View your account details.
- **Change Password**: Update your security credentials.
- **Sign Out**: Log out of the application securely.
